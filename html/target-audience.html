Target Audience

The target audience for this website would be an early or middle aged adult with an average income. They would need enough education to understand basic weather terminology. The average user would visit the website from a mobile device.

Personas

Persona 1

Max Davis is a 42 year old man and married with two children. He likes to be prepared, orderly, and to know things ahead of time. He works in an office, likes taking his family out to events, and organizes the soccer team for his children.

Persona 2

Savannah Richards is a 26 year old single woman with no kids. She is interning as a graphic designer, and commonly uses the train and buses around her city. She likes knowing the weather to know whether to wait outside at the bus stop or underground at the train station.

Scenarios

  • Is there any chance of precipitation on Friday or Saturday?
  • Will it rain tomorrow morning?
  • Will it snow within the next three days?
  • Will I need a jacket tomorrow?
  • Is the temperature going down or going up this week?
